TENNIS GAME

Rules Of The Game

  • Welcome to Tennis Game!!
  • This game is a 2-player mode game. The left player and the right player.
  • Below you will find the controls on how to play the game.

CONTROLS

Left Player

    W (Jump)
    A (Move Left)
    D (Move Right)
    Q (Hit)

Right Player

    ↑ (Jump)
    ← (Move Left)
    → (Move Right)
    L (Hit)
